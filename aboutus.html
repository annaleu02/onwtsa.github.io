What is SCAT?

We have designed the Student Codable Aquatic Traveler or S-CAT. It is a small machine that has a ARDUINO, Camera, and any sensors you want. Its ultimate goal is to allow students to program their own robot so that they can learn through hands-on engineering. The S-CAT has four propeller mounts and an attachment for an ethernet cable to control the robot. It is an individually battery powered device that provides video of the S-CATâ€™s journey when connected to a computer. The camera is always filming through livestream footage. The goal is that students, with this device, will gain a newfound interest in marine science and engineering.
